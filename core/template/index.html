Get more productive

Fotin.io is a product collecting screen usage relating to productivity. The data is sent to a rasberry pi, calculating your productivity based on activity, the LED colour turns green if you are productive and red when you're not.

Get Started!

Features

Real-Time Calculation

If you're shifting away from your work, the colour slowly changes, helping you notice that you're not as much productive.

Market Analysis

The product is unique in its implementation and can be used to boost efficiency.

User Privacy

We respect our users privacy, so the data remains local on your pc and raspberry pi.

Set Your Rules

You can set an exclusion list of programs and websites which you use in order to improve analytics and calculations.

Customer Satistified

You can look back at the analytics on how productive you were, by looking at the points where you were unproductive, the user can get a better understanding and make better time management.

Plug & Play

Just as simple as plug and play, plug your rasberry pi connected with LEDs and setup on the prompted page.
